About the Map

This interactive map shows IAM and cybersecurity job opportunities across the globe. Click on markers to see job details. Jobs are color-coded by type and marker size indicates the number of jobs at that location.

Features

  • Interactive markers with job details
  • Clustering for locations with multiple jobs
  • Color coding by job type
  • Real-time data from aggregated sources
  • Zoom and pan to explore regions
